Our story

Built for busy parents and caring hands

Smart Daycare exists to close the gap between drop-off and pick-up — so a parent's day never feels like a black box, and a caregiver's care never goes unseen.

Real-time daily reports

Every meal, nap, and activity is logged the moment it happens — so a parent at work can glance at their phone and know their child had a good lunch and a long nap, without a single phone call.

Attendance without the clipboard

Check-ins and check-outs are timestamped automatically, so classrooms always know exactly who's present, who's expected, and who needs a follow-up call — no more paper sign-in sheets that go missing.

One dashboard, every role

Parents see their own children. Caregivers see their assigned classroom. Admins see the whole center. Everyone gets exactly the view they need — nothing more, nothing hidden.

Transparency

Nothing about your child's day is a mystery.

Simplicity

Caregivers log in seconds, not paperwork.

Trust

Every record is timestamped and accountable.
